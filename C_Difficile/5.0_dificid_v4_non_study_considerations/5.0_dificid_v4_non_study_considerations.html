Non-study considerations
Considerations below were not a part of the independent evaluation: Cost-effectiveness of
treatment regimens for Clostridium difficile infection—an evaluation of the 2018 Infectious
Diseases Society of America guidelines.
Recommendation of vancomycin for severe CDI
The primary study that drove the estimate for vancomycin cure in severe CDI was a
randomized controlled trial of fidaxomicin to vancomycin for CDI, with severe CDI
sub-group sample sizes of:
  • Fidaxomicin: 112
  • Vancomycin: 123
In addition, 2 of the studies cited by the authors defined severe CDI differently:
  • Louie et al. The presence of ≥10 unformed bowel movements per day or a white-cell
    count of ≥15,001 per cubic millimeter)
  • Cornely et al. >15 000 white blood cells per μL, serum creatinine concentration >1·5
    mg/dL, or body temperature >38.5°C
Recommendation of FMT for subsequent recurrence
Costs for FMT were taken from CMS and published studies. FMT preparation costs
ranged from $226.54-$485.
After the study was published FMT preparation costs increased by 300% to 600%:
  • FMT Upper or Lower Delivery: $1595 per dose (1 bottle)
  • FMT Capsule: $1950 per dose (1 bottle = 30 capsules)
